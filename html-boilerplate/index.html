What is it all about?

Why do we care about subcultures? Why care about anything at all?

Often I do wonder why we bother diversifying ourselves, but it really is important.

Understanding Subcultures

A Subculture is a smaller community within a bigger community. For example, manual drivers are a smaller community within a community of car drivers. Another example might be a white collar worker, which is a smaller community from workers. It's kind of abstract, but I think you get the point!
